# 🏆 Tata Technologies InnoVent 2027 Project

EcoEdge AI has been developed as an innovation project for **Tata Technologies InnoVent 2027** under the category:

**AI at the Edge Solutions for Industrial Heavy Machinery**

### Selected Subcategory

**Edge AI for Sustainable & Energy-Efficient Industrial Systems**

The project focuses on enabling industrial organizations to optimize energy consumption, reduce operational costs, improve sustainability performance, and support intelligent business decision-making through modern analytics and AI.

---

# 🌍 Vision

Industries worldwide face increasing pressure to reduce energy costs, improve operational efficiency, and meet sustainability goals. Despite generating vast amounts of operational data, many organizations lack intelligent systems capable of converting raw energy data into strategic business insights.

EcoEdge AI bridges this gap by combining modern Data Engineering pipelines, Machine Learning models, and Business Intelligence dashboards into a unified Energy Intelligence Platform.

The platform enables industrial decision-makers to:

✅ Monitor energy consumption

✅ Predict future energy demand

✅ Detect abnormal usage patterns

✅ Estimate carbon emissions

✅ Identify cost-saving opportunities

✅ Improve ESG and sustainability performance

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Industrial facilities consume enormous amounts of energy across manufacturing units, heavy machinery operations, utilities, and production lines.

Common challenges include:

* Rising energy expenditure
* Energy wastage due to inefficient operations
* Lack of visibility into consumption trends
* Delayed detection of abnormal energy usage
* Difficulty forecasting future energy demand
* Increasing sustainability and ESG reporting requirements

Traditional monitoring systems often provide historical reports but fail to deliver predictive and actionable intelligence.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

EcoEdge AI is a Data-Driven Energy Intelligence Platform designed to transform industrial energy management.

Instead of relying on hardware-intensive IoT infrastructure, the platform leverages existing industrial datasets and modern analytics technologies to provide:

## Intelligent Monitoring

Track and analyze energy usage across facilities and operational units.

## Predictive Intelligence

Forecast future energy consumption using machine learning models.

## Anomaly Detection

Identify unusual energy patterns before they impact operations.

## Sustainability Analytics

Monitor carbon emissions and sustainability KPIs.

## Business Decision Support

Deliver executive-level insights through interactive Power BI dashboards.

# 🔄 Data Engineering Pipeline

The foundation of EcoEdge AI is a scalable Data Engineering architecture.

## Stage 1 — Data Ingestion

Industrial energy datasets are imported into Databricks for processing.

### Data Sources

* Energy Consumption Records
* Production Metrics
* Machine Utilization Data
* Environmental Variables
* Operational Schedules

---

## Stage 2 — Data Quality Processing

Raw industrial data often contains inconsistencies.

Cleaning operations include:

* Missing Value Treatment
* Duplicate Removal
* Invalid Record Detection
* Outlier Identification
* Data Standardization

---

## Stage 3 — Feature Engineering

Important business features are generated.

### Time Features

* Hour
* Day
* Month
* Week
* Shift

### Operational Features

* Production Volume
* Machine Category
* Utilization Rate

### Sustainability Features

* Carbon Emissions
* Energy Intensity
* Cost Metrics

# 🤖 Artificial Intelligence Layer

EcoEdge AI integrates multiple AI approaches.

## Module 1 — Energy Demand Forecasting

### Objective

Predict future industrial energy consumption.

### Algorithms

* Random Forest Regressor
* XGBoost Regressor

### Inputs

* Historical Energy Usage
* Machine Type
* Production Volume
* Temperature
* Shift Information

### Outputs

* Hourly Forecast
* Daily Forecast
* Monthly Forecast

### Business Value

Organizations can:

* Plan energy procurement
* Reduce peak demand charges
* Optimize operational schedules

---

## Module 2 — Anomaly Detection

### Objective

Detect unusual energy behavior automatically.

### Algorithm

Isolation Forest

### Detects

* Sudden Energy Spikes
* Unexpected Consumption Surges
* Operational Inefficiencies
* Potential Equipment Issues

### Business Value

Early detection reduces:

* Energy waste
* Maintenance costs
* Operational disruptions
